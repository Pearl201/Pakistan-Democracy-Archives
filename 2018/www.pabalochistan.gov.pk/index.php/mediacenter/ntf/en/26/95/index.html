12th March 2010  |  PAB/Legis:II (16) /2010

16th Session of Balochistan Provincial Assembly Summoned

NOTIFICATION


 

No. PAB/Legis: II (16)/2010.         In exercise of the powers conferred on me by clause (b) of Article 109 of the Constitution of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, 1973, Governor Balochistan, Nawab Zulfiqar Ali Magsi, has been pleased to summon the Provincial Assembly of Balochistan to meet in Provincial Assembly Hall, Shara-e-Zarghoon, Quetta, on Monday, 15th March, 2010 at 11:00 A.M.

 

(MOHAMMAD AZAM DAWI)

Additional Secretary Legislation,

Balochistan Provincial Assembly

Government Printing,                     Gazette date and supply ten copies

Press,Quetta.                                   thereof to this Secretariat.
