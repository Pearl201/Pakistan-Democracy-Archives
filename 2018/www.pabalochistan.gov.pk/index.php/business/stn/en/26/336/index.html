List of Business
Order of the Day for the meeting of the Assembly to be held on Tuesday 23rd  February, 2010 at 11:00 A.M.

1.      Recitation of Holy Qur'an.

2.      Questions
Questions asked and the answers will be given in separate list.

3.      Government Business.

RESOLUTION No.44 BY MIR ZAHOOR AHMED BULEDI, PROVINCIAL MINISTER.

            During the previous government an agreement between govt of Pakistan and Iran had reached for electricity supply to Mekran. Under this accord Iran is providing 35 MW electricity to Mekran. The present government has started providing electricity to the villages and under different development funds transmission lines have been laid down upto most of the villages of Mekran, which has increased power consumption upto 50 MW. In the upcoming days load shedding will increase manyfold, causing negative effects on routine life and the agriculture sector as well. Under the agreement it was also decided that government of Iran will also provide electricity to the areas adjacent to Balochistan-Iran border that include Tehsil Zamran, Prom etc. Govt of Iran has laid down transmission lines upto its border but government of Pakistan has still not started work on it. This house, therefore calls upon the provincial government to contact the federal government for renewal of the agreement with the government of Iran, under which electricity may be provided to Mekran. Moreover, QESCO officials may also be directed to commence their work as soon as possible, so as people of the far flung areas of Balochistan province could be able to take benefit of electricity facility.


Secretary
Balochistan Provincial Assembly
Quetta
Dated 22nd February, 2010
