Mr. Muhammad Akbar Mengal S/O Haji Mir Naik Muhammad Mengal born on March 15, 1973. He obtained the degree of B.E (Civil Engineering). An agriculturist, who visited England and Gulf Countries. He has been elected as Member Provincial Assembly of the Balochistan in General Elections 2002. His father Haji Mir Naik Muhammad was a member of the Balochistan Provincial Assembly in 1977 and his brother Mir Naseer Mengal was member of Assembly in 1985.
