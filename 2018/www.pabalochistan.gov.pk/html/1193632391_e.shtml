Home > About Assembly > Secretariat
Printer Friendly

The staff of the Assembly Secretariat totals 421 personnel, of which 96 are provided for the maintenance of the MPA Hostel. The Secretariat is divided into Administrative and Parliamentary structures, each headed by a Joint Secretary, who report to the Secretary of the Assembly, the chief administrator. The Assembly has more or less two dozen Sections of administration: Reporting, Library, Research, Legislation, Question, Translation, Committee, Printing, Editing, Accounts are the main streams.
