Printer Friendly

The new website of the provincial Assembly of Balochistan provides opportunities both for the Members and the people to monitor how the Assembly is working.

For the first time in Balochistan’s legislative history, the website offers a platform where complete information about the Assembly, its staff and working, and the honorable Members is available. 

Apart from a step towards greater transparency, I believe the website will help to bring the legislators closer to their constituents, allowing a new understanding of each other that will strengthen democracy.     

I will also acknowledge and appreciate the support of the USAID-Pakistan Legislative Strengthening Project (PLSP) in the development of the website.

Mohammad Aslam Bhootani

Speaker of the Balochistan Provincial Assembly
