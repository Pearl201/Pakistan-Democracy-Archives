Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy for this website is to be read and used in connection with the Terms & Conditions which are also published on this website.

This privacy policy has been formulated to inform site visitors how their personal information will be treated when they visit the website.

Please note that Provincial Assembly of Balochistan may amend this policy from time to time without prior notice. Site visitors are advised to check this Policy on regular basis.

Collection of Personally Identifiable Information

Whenever a User visits Website, the Website hosting server will make a record of User IP Address, the date and time of User visit, the type of Internet Browser User uses and the URL of any web site which referred User to this Website. Unless otherwise stated Cookies used (if any) in any part of Website will not be deployed for collecting personally identifiable information.

Types of Personally Identifiable Information Collected 

At times, site visitor may be required to give her/his personally identifiable information including, but not limited to, name, email address, city etc..

Disclosure of Information

User/Customer information will not be shared with, or transferred to organizations outside the Provincial Assembly of Balochistan.
