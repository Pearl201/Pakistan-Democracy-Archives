Standing Committees

Following are the Standing Committees of the Assembly:

Standing Committee on Food and AgricultureStanding Committee on Food and Agriculture is basically responsible for the legislation about following provincial government departments:
  1. Agriculture Department
  2. Food Department
  3. Irrigation and Power Development Department
  4. Cooperatives Department
Standing Committee on General AdministrationStanding Committee on General Administration play its role in legislative business of the following government departments:
  1. Services and General Adminis   Department
  2. Home, Tribal Affairs and Tourism Department
  3. Law department
  4. Information Department
Standing Committee on Industries, Labour, Education, Health and Local GovernmentStanding Committee on Industries, Labour, Education, Health and Local Government is responsible to work on the legislative process related to:
  1. Industries, Commerce and Mineral Development Department
  2. Labour Department
  3. Education Department
  4. Health Department
  5. Basic Democracies, Social Welfare and Local Government Department
Standing Committee on Revenue, Finance and DevelopmentStanding Committee on Revenue, Finance and Development is responsible to do legislation related to following government departments:
  1. Auquaf Department
  2. Colonies Department
  3. Excise and Taxation Department
  4. Land Utilization Department
  5. Revenue Department
  6. Settlement and Rehabilitation Department
  7. Transport Department
  8. Finance Department
  9. Planning and Development Department
  10. Communication and Works Department
  11. Any other department not mentioned above
