Message from Deputy Speaker

It gives me great pleasure to know that the Balochistan Provincial Assembly website is up and running and is providing a great opportunity for the general public and the honorable members to access the relevant information at a click.  The information regarding the Assembly business and members' profile etc. on the web makes it convenient and efficient for everyone to go through in a matter of very short time.  This will further enhance and strengthen the democratic institutions and will keep the general public aware about the processes and workings of the Assembly.

 I would like to congratulate the USAID for the development of  a valuable data resource for all the concerned people around the globe.
