 Mir Habib-ur-Rehman Mohammad Hasani

PB 47-Washuk		  
Minister: B - WASA
Master of Public Administration
Businessperson
Like Minded Group   (See other MPAs of this party)
Mir Dost Mohammad Mohammad Hasani
Muhammad Hassni House Kharan.
House No. 53-Green Town Sariab Road Quetta.
Mir Habib-ur-Rehman Mohammad Hasni s/o Mir Dost Mohammad Mohammad Hasni, was born in 1978 in Washuk Kharan. He belongs to a landlord family. He has done Masters in Public Administration. He was the Minister for Prison and Human Rights, Balochistan and was affiliated with the National Alliance and was elected form PB-27 Kharan-II in the general election of 2002. He has visited China, Qatar, Abu Dhabi and Dubai.
Balochi, Brahvi, Urdu
