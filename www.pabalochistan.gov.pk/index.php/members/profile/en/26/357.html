Syed Maulana Abdul Bari Agha s/o Syed Hajji Abdul Ali Agha was born 1967 in Ajaibzai District Pishin, Balochistan.  He has his degree from Wafaq-ul-Madaris Multan, Pakistan. Previously he has been Member of Balochistan Provincial Assembly in 1990 and 1993. In 2002 general election he was elected the Member of Balochistan Provincial Assembly for third time from PB-10 Pishin-III. He is affiliated with JUI (F) . He has visited Sri Lanka, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Afghanistan. He resigned on October 02, 2007 on the call of APDM.
