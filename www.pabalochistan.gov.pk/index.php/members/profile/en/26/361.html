Mr. Sardar Masood Ali Khan Luni s/o Mohammad Tahir Khan Luni was born in 1961 in Loralai. He has done his graduation in 1980.  Previously he has held the position of Chairman Loralai District Council and District Zakat Loralai for four years. He was affiliated with PML (Q). He was elected from PB-14 Loralai-I in 2002.He was the Minister for Mines & Minerals Balochistan.
