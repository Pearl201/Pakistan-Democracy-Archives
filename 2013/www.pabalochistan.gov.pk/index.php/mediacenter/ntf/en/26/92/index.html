31st December 2009  |  No. PAB/Legis: II (14)/2010.

14th Session of Balochistan Provincial Assembly Summoned

       (14th SESSION)

BALOCHISTAN PROVINCIAL ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT

 

                                     

NOTIFICATION.

 

            Dated Quetta the 31st   December, 2009.

 

 

No. PAB/Legis: II (14)/2010.         In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of Article 109 of the Constitution of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, 1973, the Governor Balochistan, has been pleased to summon the Provincial Assembly of Balochistan to meet in the Provincial Assembly Hall, Shara-e-Zarghoon, Quetta, on Monday the 4th January, 2010 at 11:00 A.M.

                                                                                                                                                                                               

 

 

 

                                                                        (MUHAMMAD KHAN MENGAL)

                                                                                                 Secretary,

                                                             Balochistan Provincial Assembly

 

 

The Controller,                                 For immediate publication in today’s

Government Printing,                     Gazette date and supply ten copies

Press, Quetta.                                   thereof to this Secretariat.
